Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 14, 2026

Swipey Cleaner ("Swipey", "we", "our", or "the app") is developed and operated by Çağatay. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information.

1. Data we collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Swipey operates entirely on your device. No photos, metadata, usage data, or personal information ever leaves your iPhone.

2. Photo library access

Swipey requests access to your photo library solely to scan for similar, duplicate, and blurry photos. All scanning and analysis is performed locally on your device using Apple's Vision framework. No images are uploaded to any server.

3. Device storage

Swipey reads your device's storage capacity and usage to display storage statistics. This information is never transmitted off your device.

4. Purchases

In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We do not have access to your payment information, Apple ID, or purchase history beyond what Apple provides to developers through App Store Connect analytics (aggregated, anonymous data).

5. Advertising

Swipey may display rewarded advertisements through Google AdMob in certain regions. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data as described in Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device's Settings under Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising.

6. Analytics

We do not use any third-party analytics services. We do not track your behavior, usage patterns, or any other activity within the app.

7. Data storage

Scan results and preferences are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms (UserDefaults and the app's Documents directory). This data is removed when you uninstall the app.

8. Children's privacy

Swipey is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
